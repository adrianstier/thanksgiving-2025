Thursday

8:00 AM
Take turkey out of fridge. Let come to room temperature.
9:30 AM
Spatchcock turkey. Prepare herb mayo coating.
10:00 AM
Preheat oven to 450°F. Rub turkey with herb mayo.
10:30 AM
Turkey goes in at 450°F for 30 min, then reduce to 400°F.
12:00 PM
Check turkey temp. Looking for 165°F in thigh.
12:30 PM
Turkey done. Rest for 30 minutes under foil.
12:45 PM
Oven to 375°F. Root vegetables and stuffing go in.
1:15 PM
Mac and cheese in oven. Reheat mashed potatoes on stove.
1:45 PM
Reheat gravy. Cook green beans.
2:15 PM
Remove everything from oven. Assemble salad.
2:30 PM
Carve turkey. Transfer to serving dishes.
3:00 PM
Dinner is served!
