Friday Leftovers

November 28th • Transform Yesterday's Feast

Leftover Ideas

Turn your Thanksgiving leftovers into new meals.

Turkey Sandwich

Uses: Turkey, Cranberry Sauce, Stuffing

The classic day-after sandwich. Layer turkey, stuffing, and cranberry on good bread.

Toast bread, spread mayo and cranberry sauce, pile high with turkey and stuffing. Optional: add gravy for dipping.

Turkey Soup

Uses: Turkey, Vegetables, Gravy

Simmer the carcass for stock, add leftover vegetables and turkey meat.

Cover turkey bones with water, simmer 2 hours. Strain, add diced turkey and any leftover veggies. Season to taste.

Thanksgiving Bowl

Uses: Everything

Layer all the leftovers in a bowl, top with gravy.

Mashed potatoes on bottom, then turkey, stuffing, vegetables. Drizzle with warm gravy. Microwave until hot.

Turkey Quesadillas

Uses: Turkey, Cheese

Shredded turkey and cheese in a crispy tortilla.

Layer cheese and turkey in tortilla, cook in skillet until golden and cheese melts. Serve with cranberry sauce for dipping.

Stuffing Waffles

Uses: Stuffing, Gravy

Press stuffing in a waffle iron for crispy outsides.

Mix stuffing with an egg, press into waffle iron until crispy. Top with turkey and gravy.

Turkey Pot Pie

Uses: Turkey, Vegetables, Gravy

Combine leftovers under a flaky crust.

Mix turkey, vegetables, and gravy. Pour into baking dish, top with puff pastry or pie crust. Bake at 400°F until golden.

Turkey Hash

Uses: Turkey, Potatoes

Crispy breakfast hash with diced turkey and potatoes.

Dice potatoes and turkey, fry in butter until crispy. Top with a fried egg.

Cranberry Turkey Salad

Uses: Turkey, Cranberry Sauce

Like chicken salad but with turkey and cranberry.

Chop turkey, mix with mayo, celery, and a spoonful of cranberry sauce. Serve on bread or greens.

Storage Tips

  • Turkey: 3-4 days in fridge, 2-3 months frozen
  • Gravy: 1-2 days in fridge, 2-3 months frozen
  • Stuffing: 3-4 days in fridge, 1 month frozen
  • Mashed Potatoes: 3-5 days in fridge, freeze poorly
  • Cranberry Sauce: 10-14 days in fridge
  • Let food cool before refrigerating
  • Store in shallow containers for faster cooling
