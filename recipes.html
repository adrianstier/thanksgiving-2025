Charcuterie Board

Prep: 30 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • Cheeses (pick 3-4): aged cheddar, brie, goat cheese, gruyere, cranberry Wensleydale, smoked gouda
  • Meats (pick 3-4): prosciutto, salami, sopressata, smoked turkey, chorizo, capicola
  • Crackers: water crackers, rosemary crackers, baguette slices, crostini
  • Fruits: apple slices, pear slices, grapes, fresh figs, dried apricots, dried cranberries
  • Nuts: candied pecans, marcona almonds, spiced nuts
  • Spreads: whole grain mustard, honey, fig jam, cranberry chutney
  • Extras: cornichons, olives, cherry tomatoes, dark chocolate, honeycomb

Assembly

  1. Prep cheeses: Pull from fridge 30-60 min before serving. Cut into wedges or slices.
  2. Arrange anchors: Place cheese wedges and small bowls (for honey, olives, etc.) first.
  3. Add meats: Fold or roll meats and place around cheeses.
  4. Fill gaps: Add crackers, fruits, and nuts in remaining spaces.
  5. Garnish: Add fresh herbs like rosemary for color.

Portions

For pre-dinner snacking: 2-3 oz cheese and 2 oz meat per person. Keep portions moderate so guests aren't full before dinner!

Spatchcock Roast Turkey

Prep: 30 min Cook: 1.5-2 hours Serves: 8-10

Ingredients

  • 12-14 lb turkey, giblets removed
  • 3 tbsp kosher salt (for dry brine)
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp mixed fresh herbs, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Dry brine (Monday): Pat turkey dry. Season all over with salt. Place uncovered on a rack in fridge for 2-3 days.
  2. Spatchcock: Cut along both sides of backbone and remove. Flip and press on breastbone to flatten.
  3. Make herb mayo: Mix mayonnaise, herbs, garlic, lemon zest, and pepper.
  4. Prep: Rub herb mayo all over, getting under the skin.
  5. Roast: 450°F for 30 min, then 400°F until thigh reaches 165°F (about 1-1.5 hrs more).
  6. Rest: 20-30 minutes before carving.

Why spatchcock?

Cooks faster, more evenly, with crispier skin. Save the backbone for stock.

Herby Mushroom Croissant Stuffing

Prep: 30 min Cook: 45 min Serves: 8-10

Ingredients

  • 6-8 large croissants, torn
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 12 oz mixed mushrooms
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups Tuscan kale, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh sage
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyere
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Toast croissants: Bake at 325°F for 15 min until dried out.
  2. Cook vegetables: Sauté mushrooms in butter until golden. Add onion, celery, garlic, kale, herbs.
  3. Combine: Mix vegetables with croissants and Gruyere. Add broth whisked with eggs.
  4. Assemble: Transfer to buttered 9x13" dish. Can refrigerate overnight.
  5. Bake: 375°F for 25-30 min until golden.

Make-ahead

Assemble night before. Add 10 min to bake time from fridge.

Roasted Root Vegetables

Prep: 20 min Cook: 40 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs carrots, chunked
  • 1 lb parsnips, chunked
  • 1 lb sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh sage leaves

Instructions

  1. Prep: Cut vegetables into similar sizes. Toss with oil and herbs.
  2. Roast: Single layer on baking sheets. 400°F for 35-40 min, stirring once.
  3. Crispy sage: Fry sage leaves in butter until crispy. Scatter over vegetables.

Martha Stewart Mac & Cheese

Prep: 20 min Cook: 30 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 6 tbsp butter, divided
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • Pinch of cayenne
  • 8 oz sharp white cheddar
  • 8 oz Gruyere
  • 6 slices white bread, cubed

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta: 2 minutes less than package directions.
  2. Make bechamel: Melt 4 tbsp butter, whisk in flour, cook 1 min. Add milk gradually. Simmer until thick.
  3. Add cheese: Off heat, stir in cheese. Add pasta.
  4. Assemble: Pour into 9x13" dish. Top with buttered bread cubes.
  5. Bake: 375°F for 30 min until golden.

Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Prep: 15 min Cook: 45 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 head garlic
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chives for garnish

Instructions

  1. Roast garlic: Cut top off, drizzle with oil, wrap in foil. 400°F for 40 min.
  2. Cook potatoes: Simmer cubed potatoes in milk and cream until tender, 20 min.
  3. Mash: Drain (save liquid). Mash with butter, sour cream, garlic. Add liquid as needed.
  4. Season: Salt, pepper, top with chives.

Make-ahead

Reheat with extra cream and butter. Can be made a day ahead.

Autumn Harvest Salad

Prep: 15 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 6 oz mixed greens
  • 2 Honeycrisp apples, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 4 oz feta, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup pepitas
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Make dressing: Whisk oil, vinegar, honey, Dijon. Can make 2 days ahead.
  2. Toast pepitas: Dry pan until fragrant.
  3. Assemble: Greens, apple, avocado, pomegranate, feta, pepitas.
  4. Dress: Just before serving.

Lemon Garlic Green Beans

Prep: 10 min Cook: 10 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs green beans, trimmed
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Blanch: Boil 3 min until bright green. Shock in ice water.
  2. Sauté: Melt butter, add garlic 30 sec, add beans.
  3. Finish: Lemon zest, juice, salt, pepper, parsley.

Mushroom Gravy

Prep: 10 min Cook: 25 min Makes: 3 cups

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 8 oz cremini mushrooms
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme

Instructions

  1. Cook mushrooms: In butter until golden, 8 min. Add shallots, garlic.
  2. Make roux: Sprinkle flour, stir 1 min.
  3. Add liquid: Wine, scrape bits. Add broth, thyme. Simmer 10-15 min.
  4. Season: Salt and pepper.

Make-ahead

Make 2 days ahead. Reheat with splash of broth.

Cinnamon Apple Crumble Cake

Prep: 30 min Cook: 50 min Serves: 10

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 apples, sliced
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar (crumble)
  • 4 tbsp cold butter (crumble)

Instructions

  1. Make crumble: Mix flour, brown sugar, cinnamon. Cut in butter.
  2. Make batter: Beat butter and sugar. Add eggs, vanilla. Alternate flour and sour cream.
  3. Assemble: Half batter in 10" springform, layer apples, rest of batter, top with crumble.
  4. Bake: 350°F for 45-50 min.
  5. Serve: Warm with vanilla ice cream.

Make-ahead

Bake day before. Warm in low oven before serving.
